Meet Our Team

   

At **Silver Fox Dynamics**, we believe that great software comes from passionate people working together with creativity and dedication. Our team is focused on building solutions that empower businesses worldwide. Here’s the amazing crew behind the magic:

Abdul Mughees

(Director General)

   

Our **Director General** is a well-qualified, approachable, and inspirational leader. With years of industry experience, Mr. Mughees is an expert in combining professionalism with a people-first approach. He focuses on inspiring, guiding, and supporting the team to deliver their best, ensuring Silver Fox Dynamics grows into a dynamic, client-focused company. His leadership is built on trust, integrity, and a clear vision for the future.

 
   
   

Fakhir Ali

(Manager – Mobile Apps Development)

   

Fakhir is our talented **Manager of Mobile Apps Development**. He excels at turning concepts into user-friendly, reliable, and engaging mobile solutions for both **Android and iOS**. With an eye for detail and a dedication to quality, he ensures every app is functional and aesthetically appealing. Fakhir empowers his team to collaborate and innovate, delivering world-class applications that solve real-world problems.

 
   
   

Amber Shan

(Manager – Web Development)

   

Meet Amber Shan, our creative and skilled **Manager of Web Development**. Amber blends strong frontend and backend expertise to build powerful, beautiful, and responsive websites. She is focused on creating a superior **user experience**, ensuring every digital platform effectively communicates the client's brand. Amber fosters a collaborative atmosphere where fresh ideas and innovation are key to creating lasting digital impressions.

 
   
   

Saad Ali

(Manager – E-Commerce Store Development)

   

Saad is our enthusiastic **Manager of E-Commerce Store Development**. He specializes in building fast, secure, and user-friendly online stores across platforms like **Shopify, WooCommerce, and Magento**. His focus is on optimizing the **customer journey** and delivering stores that not only look great but also drive results. Saad's leadership ensures Silver Fox Dynamics is a trusted partner for businesses in the world of online commerce.

 
   
   

Muhammad Kashif

(Manager – ERP Development)

   

Muhammad Kashif is our highly skilled **Manager of ERP Development**. Kashif's expertise helps businesses achieve greater efficiency, clarity, and control by solving complex challenges with tailored, reliable systems. Asim Butt
