Information We Collect

We collect various types of information to provide and improve our services:

👤

Personal Information

Name, email, phone number, and billing details provided when you sign up, purchase services, or contact us.

💻

Technical Data

Device identifiers, IP address, browser type, and usage activity that help us improve functionality.

🍪

Cookies

Small files stored on your device that enhance site performance and personalize your experience.

How We Use Your Data

We use collected information only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • ✓ Delivering and maintaining our products and services.

Industry-standard encryption for all sensitive data transmission.
