Privacy Policy

At Silver Fox Dynamics, your privacy matters. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the steps we take to protect it whenever you engage with our websites, applications, or services.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: name, email, phone number, and billing details provided when you sign up, purchase services, or contact us.
  • Technical Data: device identifiers, IP address, browser type, and usage activity that help us improve functionality.
  • Cookies: small files stored on your device that enhance site performance and personalize your experience.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use collected information only for legitimate business purposes, including:

  • Delivering and maintaining our products and services.
  • Improving user experience and product performance.
  • Providing support and responding to inquiries.
  • Sharing important updates, promotions, or alerts (when you opt in).
  • Ensuring compliance with laws and preventing fraud or misuse.

3. Sharing & Disclosure

Your information is never sold. It may only be shared with:

  • Trusted partners who support our services under strict confidentiality.
  • Payment processors and third-party tools required for operations.
  • Regulatory authorities if legally required.

4. Data Protection

We use encryption, secure servers, and restricted access controls to keep your information safe against unauthorized use or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have control over your personal data, including the right to:

  • Request a copy of the information we hold.
  • Ask for corrections or updates.
  • Request deletion of your data where permitted by law.
  • Opt out of promotional emails anytime.

6. Cookies & Preferences

You can manage or disable cookies in your browser settings. Some site features may not work properly if cookies are disabled.

7. Policy Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated occasionally to reflect changes in law, technology, or business practices. Any changes will be posted here with an updated effective date.
