Privacy Policy

At Silver Fox Dynamics LLC, your privacy and trust are our highest priorities. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you interact with our products, services, websites, and applications.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal Information: such as name, email address, phone number, and billing details when you register, contact us, or purchase services.
  • Technical Information: including IP addresses, browser type, device identifiers, and usage logs to enhance user experience.
  • Cookies & Tracking: small data files that help us understand your preferences and improve functionality.

2. How We Use Your Information

We process collected data responsibly and only for legitimate purposes, such as:

  • Delivering and improving our software products and services.
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries.
  • Sending updates, promotional offers, or service-related notices (with your consent).
  • Ensuring security, fraud prevention, and compliance with applicable laws.

3. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We respect your confidentiality and do not sell your data. We may share information only with:

  • Trusted partners who help us deliver services under strict confidentiality agreements.
  • Regulatory authorities if legally required.
  • Third-party tools (such as payment processors) strictly for operational needs.

4. Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures, including encryption, secure servers, and access controls, to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You have full control over your personal information. This includes:

  • Accessing and reviewing the data we hold about you.
  • Requesting corrections or updates to your details.
  • Requesting deletion of your data, where applicable.
  • Opting out of promotional communications at any time.

6. Cookies & Preferences

Our websites may use cookies to enhance your browsing experience. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect some features of our services.

7. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in laws, practices, or technology. Updated policies will always be available on this page with a revised effective date.
